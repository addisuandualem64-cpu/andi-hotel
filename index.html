Our Story

About Us

Etetu Abusha Hotel

A Home in the
Heart of Ethiopia

"We don't just offer rooms — we offer an experience rooted in genuine Ethiopian hospitality."

Etetu Abusha Hotel stands proudly in Alaba Kulito, a vibrant town in the Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region of Ethiopia. We were founded with a simple but powerful belief: that every guest deserves to feel truly at home, no matter how far they've traveled.

Our hotel combines the warmth and generosity that defines Ethiopian culture with the comfort and reliability that modern travelers expect. From our thoughtfully furnished rooms to our restaurant serving authentic local cuisine, every detail has been considered with our guests in mind.

With a 4.3-star rating across 23 Google reviews, our reputation speaks to the dedication of our team and the quality of the experience we deliver every single day.
